My favourite line from a book

I never believed in love. Never wanted it. I didn't see the practical value and, to be honest, I was doing just fine without it. But then I met you. You're smile, your strength, your intelligence and compassion. You filled a part of my soul I always thought would be empty, and you healed scars I never knew existed. And I realized... It's not that I didn't believe in love before. It's that I was saving it all for you.
